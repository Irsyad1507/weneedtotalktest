We Need to Talk

Welcome to We Need to Talk, where we discuss any issue that we think need to be talked about. Write your post here, publish, and let the world know how important the issue is. But hold on, because we have some guidelines that you need to follow.


1. You must start your blog title with 'We need to talk about... '. Here's the starter template for you.

2. You must write your blog in the body of the post. You can use the editor here to write your blog.


3. You must not use any profanity in your blog. We have a filter that will remove any profanity from your blog.


4. You must not use any offensive language in your blog. We have a filter that will remove any offensive language from your blog.


5. You must not use any hate speech in your blog. We have a filter that will remove any hate speech from your blog.


6. You must not use any spam in your blog. We have a filter that will remove any spam from your blog.


7. Speak with knowledge, not emotions.
